Build a complete Staff Profile Management system (Phase 1 only).

Do NOT include tasks, performance, or analytics.

---

1. STAFF TYPES

---

Support 3 categories:

* ADMIN
* TELECALLER
* HOME_STAFF

---

2. DATABASE (PRISMA)

---

Create models:

Staff:

* id

* name

* phone

* email

* roleType (ADMIN / TELECALLER / HOME_STAFF)

* designation (Supervisor, Cook, Telecaller, etc.)

* homeId (relation to Home)

* status (ACTIVE / INACTIVE)

* bloodGroup

* emergencyContact1Name

* emergencyContact1Phone

* emergencyContact2Name

* emergencyContact2Phone

* createdAt

---

Home:

* id
* name (Uppal, Begumpet, etc.)
* address

---

StaffDocuments:

* id
* staffId
* type (PHOTO, AADHAR, PAN, APPOINTMENT, EXPERIENCE)
* fileUrl
* createdAt

---

3. BACKEND (NESTJS)

---

Create APIs:

Staff:

* GET /staff
* GET /staff/:id
* POST /staff
* PATCH /staff/:id
* DELETE /staff/:id

Documents:

* POST /staff/upload-document
* GET /staff/:id/documents

Homes:

* GET /homes
* POST /homes

---

4. FRONTEND (NEXT.JS)

---

A. Staff List Page

* Display all staff
* Filters:

  * Role Type
  * Home
  * Designation

---

B. Add Staff Form

Fields:

* Name
* Phone
* Email
* Role Type (dropdown)
* Designation
* Home (dropdown)
* Blood Group
* Emergency Contact 1 (name + phone)
* Emergency Contact 2 (name + phone)

---

C. Staff Profile Page

Sections:

1. Basic Info

* Name
* Role Type
* Designation
* Home

2. Medical & Emergency

* Blood Group
* Emergency Contacts

3. Documents

* Upload and view:

  * Photo
  * Aadhar
  * PAN
  * Appointment Letter
  * Experience Certificate

---

5. FILE STORAGE

---

Use Supabase Storage for:

* Staff photos
* Documents

---

6. ROLE ACCESS

---

FOUNDER / ADMIN:

* Can create, edit, delete staff

STAFF:

* View only (optional)

---

7. UI REQUIREMENTS

---

* Use existing theme (dark + orange)
* Clean layout
* Simple navigation

---

8. IMPORTANT

---

* Do NOT implement:

  * Tasks
  * Performance tracking
  * Salary system

---

## GOAL:

* Fully working staff profiles
* Documents upload + view
* Emergency data saved
* Home-wise staff mapping ready
