==================================================
PART 2 — ACTION TYPES
==================================================

Generate action items from existing Meal Sponsorship data.

At minimum support these action types:

1. MEAL_COMPLETION_PENDING
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND mealCompleted is null or false

2. DONOR_VISIT_PENDING
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND donorVisited is null
- AND mealCompleted is true or meal date has passed

3. BALANCE_PENDING
Show when:
- effectiveBalance > 0

effectiveBalance =
totalAmount - (amountReceived + postMealAmountReceived)

4. PROMISE_FOLLOWUP_PENDING
Show when:
- promiseMade = true
- AND promiseNotes is not empty

5. THANK_YOU_PENDING
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND thankYouSent is null or false

6. REVIEW_PENDING
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND reviewRequested is null or false

7. ASK_HI_PENDING
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND askedToSendHi is null or false

8. EXTRA_ITEMS_NOT_RECORDED
Show when:
- mealServiceDate is today or in the past
- AND extraItemsGiven is null
This means staff has not yet recorded whether donor brought anything extra.

IMPORTANT:
Do not create duplicate action rows unnecessarily.
One meal sponsorship may generate multiple different action types if several follow-ups are still pending.
