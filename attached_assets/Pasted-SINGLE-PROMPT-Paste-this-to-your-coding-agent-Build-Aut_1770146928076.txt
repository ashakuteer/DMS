Special Days logic

Because year is not stored (only MM-DD), compute the “next occurrence date” (this year or next year).

Determine daysUntil.

If daysUntil is in {30,15,7,2,0} then queue email.

Also handle missed cases: if job was down and today is e.g. 6 days before, you may send the closest pending one (optional) BUT default is strict to those offsets only.

Do not spam daily: max 1 email per pledge per day; use EmailLog (relatedId + date) or a rule: send overdue reminders only every 3 days.
